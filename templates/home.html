Our Purpose

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To become a leading centre of excellence in VLSI education and semiconductor training, empowering students with advanced technical knowledge, innovation, and practical expertise to contribute to the global semiconductor industry.

We aspire to create a learning environment where students develop strong design skills, research mindset, and industry readiness, enabling them to build successful careers in VLSI design and chip development.

Our Mission

To empower aspiring engineers with strong knowledge and practical skills in VLSI and semiconductor technologies. We strive to provide industry-oriented training, hands-on project experience, and expert mentorship that prepare students to meet the evolving demands of the global semiconductor industry.

We aim to bridge the gap between academic learning and real-world VLSI design practices through training in RTL design, FPGA development, ASIC design flow, and physical design methodologies.
